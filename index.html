About Michael

Ever since I was a young boy I was interested in tech, video games, and the internet. I remember building hobby websites using nothing more than Notepad and some shoddily cobbled HTML code uploaded to Angelfire and Geocities. This was back before Facebook or even MySpace, where regular people first experienced creating a "Web Presence". I haven't bought a pre-built computer in over 20 years as I prefer to build them myself. That way, I can pick each part individually and I also enjoy the process.

I'm a proud nerd and an enormous Star Wars fan, to the point that I taught myself small electronics soldering so that I could build my own replica lightsabers out of parts. I've gutted cheap, name-brand toy lightsabers just to harvest the soundboard from them to put into something a bit more powerful.
